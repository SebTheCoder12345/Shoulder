Phase 2 → Phase 3 criteria
Pain at rest 0/10 · rallying pain ≤ 1/10 · all Phase 2 exercises at full load · osteopath confirms back contracture improving. Minimum 6 weeks.

Stop immediately if pain exceeds 1/10. Return to Phase 2 mobility for 48h before retrying.
